My Favorite TV Detectives

Robin Griffin

Name: Robin Griffin

Title: Detective

Series: “Top of the Lake”

Run dates: 2013-2017

Elisabeth Moss plays Robin Griffin, a Sydney detective specializing in assault. As a detective specializing in crimes that involve abuse (and a abuse survivor herself), she’s a less cartoonish version of Law & Order: SVU's Olivia Benson. She’s tough and capable, beat-up and broken. She’s made of china and steel. She’s difficult to love but impossible to ignore. (Source: BuzzFeed)

Watch streaming: Hulu
